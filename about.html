The Beginning

A single vision,
obsessively refined

HOTEL was founded by architect Elise Montclair in 2012 with one unwavering conviction: that a hotel could be a living artwork — one where every texture, temperature, and transition had been considered as deeply as any museum installation.

The building itself was a century-old private bank. Montclair stripped it to its limestone bones and rebuilt the interior from platinum-toned stone, hand-blown glass, and metals drawn from a single foundry in Zurich.

We employ no standard of luxury. We pursue only the singular, the specific, the quietly astonishing.
